About Me

I'm a freelance photographer based in Dublin, Ireland. Specialising in automotive photography, this covers everything from motorsport dynamic panning shots to detailed close-ups.

I also enjoy documenting my travels through the lens and creating virtual photography in my spare time, which lets me explore lighting, scenes, and compositions in ways that aren’t always possible in real life.
