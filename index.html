About Me


I'm a freelance photographer based in Dublin, Ireland. Specialising in automotive photography, this covers everything from motorsport dynamic panning shots to detailed close-ups. Whether it's in motion or at a standstill, I strive to capture the unique character of each vehicle.

In my spare time, I also enjoy creating virtual photography. It allows me to explore scenes, lighting and compositions that might not be possible in the real world.
